This is a mobile-first app prototype in Brazilian Portuguese (pt-BR) for a property inspection platform called vistor.ia.

## Product Vision

vistor.ia is an AI-powered mobile app designed for real estate property inspections (vistoria de entrada e saída) in Brazil. The app should feel modern, efficient, and reduce manual typing by leveraging voice and camera inputs.

The design must prioritize:

- Speed of use in the field
- Clarity of information
- Minimal typing
- Strong visual hierarchy
- Offline-friendly UX patterns

## Platform & Layout
- Mobile-first (primary target: smartphones)
- Use standard mobile screen sizes (iPhone/Android)
- Bottom navigation preferred
- Large touch targets for field usability
- Clean, modern UI (inspired by apps like Uber, Notion, iFood)

## Branding & Style
- App name: vistor.ia
- Tone: professional, trustworthy, tech-enabled
- Style: minimal, clean, slightly bold

Suggested colors:
- Primary: Deep blue or indigo (trust)
- Secondary: Light gray backgrounds
- Accent: Green (success) and red/orange (issues/damage)

Use icons for:
- Camera
- Microphone
- Rooms (kitchen, bathroom, etc.)
- Alerts/issues
